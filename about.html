About Zaid Chicken

Our journey of serving the freshest, finest quality chicken to families since 2015.

Our Story

Zaid Chicken Centre was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide families with the freshest, highest-quality chicken at affordable prices. What started as a small neighborhood shop has grown into a trusted name in the community.

Our founder, Mr. Zaid Ahmad, believed that every family deserves access to premium quality poultry products. With years of experience in the industry, he established a business built on trust, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of happy customers daily, maintaining the same commitment to freshness and quality that we started with. Our state-of-the-art processing facility ensures that every piece of chicken meets the highest hygiene standards.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the Zaid Chicken Centre story.

2015

The Beginning

Zaid Chicken Centre opened its first shop with a vision to serve fresh, quality chicken to the local community.

2017

Home Delivery Launch

We introduced home delivery service, making fresh chicken accessible to customers at their doorstep.

2019

New Product Range

Launched marinated and ready-to-cook products, catering to the modern, busy lifestyle of our customers.

2021

10,000 Happy Customers

Reached the milestone of serving 10,000 satisfied customers, a testament to our quality and service.

2024

Digital Transformation

Launched our new website with online ordering, making it easier than ever to get fresh chicken delivered.
