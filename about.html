About Bismillah Chicken

Your trusted source for fresh, halal chicken in Kothur.

Our Story

Bismillah Chicken Centre was established with a simple mission: to provide the community of Kothur with the freshest, highest-quality halal chicken at honest prices.

Located near Water Tank on Penjerla Road, Kothur, we have been serving families in our community with premium quality chicken that meets the highest standards of hygiene and halal certification.

Our commitment to quality, freshness, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for families who care about what they serve to their loved ones.
